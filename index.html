Introduction

My name is Emma Faulk. I am currently a senior at the University of Georgia, and plan to graduate in May 2018. I have attened UGA for the entirety of my college career. My major is political science with a minor in Communication Studies. I am fascinated with current events and enjoy studying about them through a historical political context. At this university, I have discovered my passion for the community along with my eagerness to learn about the history of the United States.

Academic Interests

The best classes that I have taken at UGA have been Southern Politics and Great American Speeches and Speakers. These two classes have led me to know more about where I live in the South, and how our country has been influenced by communication over the course of its existence. In Southern Politics, we learned about the history of the southern United States, and how it has been shaped by Civil War Reconstruction and the Civil Rights Movement. The Great American Speeches and Speakers class allowed an inside look in the lives of people throughout history by examining famous speeches from American History. These two classes narrated an untold history of the United States, in a way that many textbooks can't detail. Each of them brought a new understanding of our country that I had not been introduced to before college.
