A LITTLE ABOUT US
Shop with a Bulldog (SWAB) is a 501(c)3 non-profit student organization at the University of Georgia that aims to bring the joy of the holiday season to children in Athens-Clarke County.
The goals of Shop with a Bulldawg are based strongly in service to the community. We believe children in Athens-Clarke County deserve to have a joyous holiday experience. Above that, we believe that these children need to build healthy relationships, learn about educational advancement, and follow positive role models in their own community.

Over the past six years, we have been able to impact the lives of close to 2,000 children.  With your help, we can make 2016 an even larger event aimed at linking arms with the community and bringing the holidays to Athens.
